Projection Learning
===================

Here are the files for reproducing the study on learning a projection matrix for
inducing hypernym from hyponyms.

* all_ru_pairs_ruwikt20160210_parsed.txt is the parsed Russian Wiktionary
* pairs-isas-aa.csv is the list of subsumptions extrated using Hearst patterns
